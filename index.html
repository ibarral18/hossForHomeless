HOSS’s mission is to provide protective wear and other hygienic products to local underprivileged and underserved communities.

What's inside a HOSS bag?

Each bag contains masks, a bottle of hand sanitizer, gloves, flushable wipes, bottles of water, a tooth brush, tooth paste, nail clippers, and deodorant.

Our vision

Our vision is to place hope in those who have no means for obtaining basic hygienic products in order to promote a safer and healthier environment for all people. We strive to distribute items of high manufacturing quality as opposed to low grade and relatively disposable, lasting items. This is a standard HOSS undertook in order to better serve the less fortunate; an essential undertaking we felt needed to be made in the nonprofit community.

How you can make
a difference

By donating funds or items, all of your proceeds will be directed towards purchasing and filling bags with hygiene products for the less fortunate.
